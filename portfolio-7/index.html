My Journey

Growing up in the rural landscapes of Madhya Pradesh, I've always felt a deep connection with nature. From a young age, I've been fascinated by the intricate balance of ecosystems and how human activities impact our environment.

As a student at Navodaya Vidhyalaya, I've had the opportunity to explore my passion for environmental science through various research projects and field studies. I'm particularly interested in sustainable agriculture, renewable energy, and water conservation techniques that can be implemented in rural communities.

My goal is to pursue higher education in Environmental Science and contribute to solving the pressing environmental challenges that our world faces today.
