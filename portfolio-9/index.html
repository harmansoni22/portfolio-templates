About Me

My Story

Hello! I'm Kavya, a 16-year-old student at Navodaya Vidhyalaya, Multhan (MP, India). I'm passionate about web development, digital design, and the intersection of technology and creativity.

My journey in coding began two years ago when I first discovered HTML and CSS. Since then, I've been constantly learning and expanding my skills. I believe that great design isn't just about aesthetics—it's about creating meaningful experiences that connect with people.

As a student, I'm dedicated to my academic pursuits while also developing practical skills that will shape my future career. I enjoy solving complex problems and finding innovative solutions that combine technical expertise with creative thinking.

Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works.

When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me reading science fiction novels, practicing digital illustration, or participating in school competitions. I believe in continuous growth and pushing the boundaries of what's possible.
